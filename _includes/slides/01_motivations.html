Talking Heads - Once In A Lifetime

How did we get here?

  • Minimizing vendor lock-in has its costs
    • Other than S3, very minimal dependency on other AWS services
    • Trade-off: Takes a lot of compute and operational knowledge to roll your own

How did we get here?

  • Incomprehensible early assumptions / design decisions
    • No process of data de-duplication
    • No process to delete dead data
  • Atypical usage patterns
    • A few outliers can turn a not great situation into a full blown nightmare
