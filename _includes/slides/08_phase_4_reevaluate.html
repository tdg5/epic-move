Phase 4: Reevaluate

Reevaluate

  • Know your bottlenecks and how to work around them
    • Different systems will present problems at different times
    • Be adaptable
    • Just keep swimming
  • Anything you can do in place and incrementally, you should
    • If at first you don't succeed, try again incrementally
  • It's going to take longer than you think
    • And definitely longer than people want to hear
    • Be prepared to cycle teams on and off the project
      • Limits fatigue and spreads knowledge
