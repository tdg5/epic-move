Hindsight is 20/20

In hindsight…

  • Start-ups are hard, but don't be afraid to:
    • Discontinue problem features
    • Renegotiate or end contracts with problem customers
  • Spend more on engineers/engineering early on
    • The mistakes cheap engineers make early on will cost you later
  • Large-scale migrations can easily become a scapegoat for various problems both technical and organizational
    • Protect against this
  • Estimation is hard
  • In many migration scenarios, the business is the customer
    • Boundaries can become blur or vanish
    • A lot of work will go into migrations and audits, but never forget:
      Deletes are the deliverable
