Phase 0: Research

Understand your situation

  • Get a sense of your various growth rates
    • Service growth rates and costs should inform order of attack
    • Different strategies required when growth outpaces migration
  • How do you define "done"?
    • When everything has been migrated?
    • When the cost of migrating outweighs the return?
      • 75% of the work will get you 90% of the benefit
  • Know your bottlenecks and trouble services
    • Optimize where possible
    • Be prepared to wait
    • Be prepared to work around these systems

Consider alternatives to migration

  • S3 Infrequent Access storage
  • Amazon Glacier
  • Amazon Snowball Import/Export service
  • Retrieve 3rd party data again
  • Do you really need all the data?
    • Even a data backup company found extraneous data

Replacements and Upgrades

  • Replacing AWS services will be harder than you expect
    • Start researching and vetting replacements ASAP
  • Determine what needs to be upgraded now
    • More recent versions tend to have better migration capabilities which may affect your design
    • Separate upgrades from migrations where possible

S3 clones != S3

  • Test your S3 replacement of choice early and often
    • Test both egress scale and steady-state scale
  • Ensure S3 replacement will support your dataset's:
    • partitioning strategy
    • typical file sizes
    • total number of files
    • total amount of storage
    • API dependencies
  • Remember: data replication is now your responsibility

Timing

  • Identify biggest contributors to current and future costs and start there
  • Alternatively, focus on data that will yield greatest return for least effort
  • Get service upgrades started early
  • Find replacement services — depending on how you've abused AWS, finding replacements may take time
  • The migration will take time, so start development there and focus on next steps once migration is underway
  • Consider building out several options simultaneously at the start in case some strategies work out better than others or don't work out at all
